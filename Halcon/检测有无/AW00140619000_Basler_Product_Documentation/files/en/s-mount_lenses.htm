S-Mount Lenses

There is a large range of lenses available to cater for different application scenarios and requirements.

Basler's S-mount lenses are available to fit image circles of 1/3" to 1/1.8". They offer focal lengths from 2.1 mm to 50 mm and have a fixed aperture within a range of F1.6 to F2.5. You focus them by adjusting how far you insert the lens in the mount. Each focal length is available either with or without an IR cut filter.

S-mount lenses are especially suitable for Basler's board level cameras with image circles of 1/3" to 1/1.8".

To find a suitable S-mount lens, visit the Basler website.
