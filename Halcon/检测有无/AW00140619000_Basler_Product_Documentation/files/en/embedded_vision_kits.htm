Embedded Vision Kits

Basler's Embedded Vision Kits provide developers with the modular components they need to evaluate Basler cameras.

This allows you to design your camera into embedded vision systems.

Besides the camera, the kits usually contain a processing board, accessories such as lens and cable, and preinstalled software.

For more information, visit the Basler website.
