Hardware Installation (dart BCON for LVDS/MIPI)

For information about installing your Basler dart BCON for LVDS or Basler dart BCON for MIPI camera module, refer to the Embedded Vision Kits documentation.

Basler dart BCON for LVDS and dart BCON for MIPI camera modules are usually evaluated using an Embedded Vision Kit.

The final hardware installation depends on your individual embedded vision solution. For general hardware design recommendations, refer to the following topics:

  • BCON for LVDS Hardware Design Guide
  • BCON for MIPI Hardware Design Guide
